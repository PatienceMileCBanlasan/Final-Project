How to Prevent Gender Inequality

Promote Equal Access to Education

Ensure that girls and boys have equal opportunities to attend and complete school. Education empowers women and girls, opens career opportunities, and helps break cycles of poverty and inequality.

Enforce Equal Pay for Work

Implement and enforce laws that require employers to pay women the same as men for doing the same work. Transparent wage policies and pay audits can help close the gender pay gap.

Challenge Gender Stereotypes and Norms

Encourage media, education systems, and communities to dismantle harmful stereotypes about what men and women “should” do. Promoting diverse role models helps normalize gender equality.

Ensure Legal Protection Against Discrimination and Violence

Enact and enforce laws protecting individuals from gender-based discrimination, harassment, and violence—both in the workplace and in society. Support for survivors and legal recourse is essential.

Support Grassroots and Community Movements

Fund and uplift local organizations and activists working on gender issues. These groups often understand their communities' specific needs and can drive sustainable change from the ground up.
